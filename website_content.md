### D. Creative Direction & Aesthetic Guidance

This content will guide the visual implementation of the two themes.

#### 1. Modern Core Aesthetic (General Events)

| Element | Guidance |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Visual Style** | Clean lines, ample white space, high-contrast black and white photography with pops of the Soft Gold accent (`#C89F5A`). Minimalist typography. Focus on the geometry and modern architecture of Marrakech (e.g., Yves Saint Laurent's gardens, modern hotels). |
| **Mood** | Sophisticated, efficient, international, and artistic. |
| **Photography Style** | Photojournalistic, clean, high-resolution, with a focus on human connection and professional execution. |

#### 2. Traditional Luxury Aesthetic (Moroccan Weddings & Engagements)

| Element | Guidance |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Visual Style** | Rich textures, deep color saturation (using the Traditional Luxury Palette), intricate patterns (zellige, mashrabiya). Use of serif fonts for headings to convey tradition and luxury. Background elements should subtly incorporate the Rich Sienna and Deep Indigo. |
| **Mood** | Romantic, opulent, deeply cultural, and timeless. |
| **Photography Style** | Cinematic, warm tones, focus on traditional attire, intricate details, and the dramatic lighting of riads and palaces. |
